Jérôme Lohéac

Jérôme Lohéac is a Senior Scientific Researcher at the Institut de Recherche en Communications et Cybernétique de Nantes, a scientific institution linked to the CNRS in France. His fields of research are Swimming of microorganisms(1); Time optimal control for PDEs(2); Optimal control of finite dimensional systems with state constraints(3); Controllability of parameter-dependent systems(4).
