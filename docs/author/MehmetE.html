Mehmet Ersoy

I am currently associate professor at the Université de Toulon (UTLN) and do my research at the Institut Mathématiques de Toulon (IMATH) -Équipe de Modélisation Numérique (MN) . My fields of specialization and research interests include Non linear analysis of PDEs, Numerical analysis in fluid mechanics. Before joining the team MN, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Basque Center for Applied Mathematics, Derio, Spain, directed by/in collaboration with Enrique Zuazua. I obtained my Ph.D in Applied Mathematics at the Laboratoire de Mathématiques-LAMA under the supervision of Christian Bourdarias & Stéphane Gerbi, Université de Savoie, Chambéry, France in 2010. I deliver Scientific Computing and Numerical Analysis courses at the Ecole d'Ingénieurs de l'Université de Toulon which offers the possibility to get high level skills.
